Why SUP?

Fitness

Paddleboarding is a great why to improve both cardiovascular fitness and strength. The nature of balancing on your board engages your full body, and longer paddles will give you a great cardio workout. You can try carrying a passenger or even practicing Yoga whilst on your SUP to take the benefits to the next level!

Mental Health

Studies have show that living close to water can have a positive effect on your mental health. Regular paddle boarding allows you to connect with water and the natural world in new ways that will boost your mood. Concentrating the mind on balance creates a meditative environment where you are able to let any stresses slip away and keep focus in the present moment.

Social

The paddleboard community is ever growing and now, more than ever people are looking to connect with others who enjoy the sport. Here at South Coast SUP we encourage this growth and look to create communal enjoyment of the sport through various group events that can be found on our events page.
