About

About South Coast SUP

South Coast SUP is Stand-Up Paddleboard club and community based in Hove, on the South Coast of Sussex.
The club was set up to encourage new and existing lovers of the sport to come together to share the joys and benefits of paddleboarding.

What we do

South Coast SUP is a platform for information on the sport and the best local places to enjoy it.
Here you can find suggested routes to take as well as sign up to one of our group events via our events page. We hope to see you on the water soon!
